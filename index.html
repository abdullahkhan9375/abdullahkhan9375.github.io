I am really excited to announce BYBank (Bring Your Bank), a project that I have been working on for quite some time.

It aims to solve a core problem that many students and mentors face. The availability of free and personalised test preparation material on the internet. It is often the case where students have to ‘buy’ repositories of questions in order to prepare themselves for their tests / exams. These platforms and many of their useful features are hidden behind subscriptions and paywalls.

My project aims to address these problems. A platform that centrally hosts user uploaded question banks, flashcards and test prep material. Students can then share their contributions with others, create personalised tests for their preparation. Essentially, you can create your own question banks, tests and solve them on the website. All of this can be shared with others, or searched among (potentially) hundreds of other banks.
